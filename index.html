Privacy Policy – PreRoll

PreRoll does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses the device camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording photos and videos initiated by the user. All media is saved locally on the user’s device.

PreRoll does not transmit any data to external servers and does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer.
